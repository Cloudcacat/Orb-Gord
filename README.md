***“Orb-Gord” listen to your heart***

  Orb-Gord, a mental health website that has a test to measure your own stress level according to the Department of Mental Health's criteria. There is a chatbot system to discuss problems or listen. There are also posts about psychological information to benefit users. Not only can it provide assistance to people with mental health problems, but also can it be used to study mental health information to assess stress levels and mental health risks from the behavior of those around us.

  Tech Stack : HTML CSS JavaScript (Vanilla) No backend required (static site)
